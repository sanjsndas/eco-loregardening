Comprehensive Gardening Services

Garden Design & Planning

Our garden design service begins with a thorough consultation to understand your vision, preferences, and the unique characteristics of your space. We consider factors like sunlight exposure, soil conditions, climate, and your desired level of maintenance to create a customized garden plan. Our designs incorporate both aesthetic and functional elements, creating outdoor spaces that are beautiful, practical, and sustainable.

We specialize in various garden styles including modern minimalist, cottage garden, Mediterranean, Japanese Zen, and native wildlife gardens. Our design process includes detailed sketches, plant selection, hardscape planning, and a phased implementation plan that works with your budget and timeline.

Plant Selection & Installation

Choosing the right plants is crucial for a thriving garden. Our horticultural experts select plants based on your local climate, soil conditions, sunlight availability, and maintenance preferences. We prioritize native and pollinator-friendly plants that support local ecosystems while also incorporating exotic species where appropriate.

Our installation process goes beyond simply putting plants in the ground. We prepare the soil with organic amendments, ensure proper spacing for mature growth, and implement water-efficient irrigation systems. For edible gardens, we use organic practices and companion planting techniques to promote healthy growth without harmful chemicals.

Hardscaping & Garden Structures

Hardscaping elements provide structure and functionality to your garden. We design and install patios, walkways, retaining walls, decks, and other structural features using sustainable materials like reclaimed wood, natural stone, and permeable pavers. Our team can also build garden structures such as pergolas, trellises, raised beds, and greenhouses.

We specialize in creating outdoor living spaces that extend your home's functionality. From cozy fire pits to elegant water features, we design hardscape elements that complement your garden's aesthetic while serving practical purposes.

Irrigation & Water Management

Efficient water use is at the heart of our gardening philosophy. We design and install smart irrigation systems that deliver the right amount of water exactly where it's needed. Our systems include rain sensors, moisture meters, and programmable controllers to prevent water waste.

For clients interested in sustainable water solutions, we specialize in rainwater harvesting systems, greywater recycling, and drought-tolerant landscaping. We can also retrofit existing irrigation systems to make them more efficient and environmentally friendly.

Organic Lawn Care

Transform your lawn into a healthy, chemical-free green space with our organic lawn care program. We use natural fertilizers, compost teas, and biological pest control to nurture your grass without synthetic chemicals. Our approach focuses on building healthy soil ecosystems that support lush turf naturally.

For clients interested in reducing their lawn area, we offer lawn conversion services, replacing grass with native groundcovers, flowering meadows, or low-maintenance ornamental grasses that require less water and care.

Seasonal Garden Maintenance

Keep your garden looking its best year-round with our comprehensive maintenance services. Our seasonal programs include pruning, fertilizing, mulching, pest management, and bed cleanup tailored to each plant's needs and the changing seasons.

We offer flexible maintenance schedules from weekly visits to seasonal tune-ups, all performed by knowledgeable gardeners who understand the specific needs of your plants. Our maintenance services also include soil testing, amendment applications, and ongoing plant health monitoring.

Specialty Gardens

Create a unique garden space tailored to your specific interests with our specialty garden services:

  • Edible Gardens: Beautiful and productive vegetable gardens, fruit trees, and herb spirals designed for maximum yield in limited spaces.
  • Pollinator Gardens: Plantings specifically designed to attract and support bees, butterflies, and other beneficial pollinators.
  • Medicinal Herb Gardens: Curated collections of healing plants with information on their traditional uses.
  • Japanese Zen Gardens: Serene spaces featuring carefully placed rocks, raked gravel, and minimalist plantings.
  • Vertical Gardens: Space-saving green walls and container systems for small urban spaces.
  • Cutting Gardens: Flower gardens designed specifically for providing beautiful blooms for indoor arrangements.

Garden Lighting

Extend the enjoyment of your garden into the evening hours with our professional landscape lighting services. We design lighting schemes that highlight your garden's best features while providing safety and security. Our energy-efficient LED systems create magical nighttime atmospheres while minimizing energy consumption.

Lighting options include path lights, uplighting for trees and architectural features, subtle deck and step lighting, and dramatic focal point lighting. We can also install smart lighting systems that you can control from your phone or program to change with the seasons.

Garden Renovation & Restoration

Breathe new life into an overgrown or outdated garden with our renovation services. We carefully assess existing plants and structures, preserving what's healthy and valuable while removing or replacing elements that no longer serve the space. Our restoration process respects the garden's history while introducing modern, sustainable practices.

For historic properties, we offer period-appropriate garden restoration services, researching original designs and using heirloom plant varieties to recreate gardens true to their era.
