Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting Ecolore and our gardening services.

How quickly can I expect a response after submitting the contact form?

We pride ourselves on prompt communication. You'll receive an automated confirmation immediately after form submission, and one of our gardening consultants will respond to your inquiry within 24 hours during our normal business hours (Monday-Friday, 9am-6pm). For inquiries submitted on weekends or holidays, we'll respond by the next business day.

What information should I include in my message to get the best response?

To help us provide the most accurate and helpful response, please include:

  • The type of service you're interested in (design, maintenance, installation, etc.)
  • Your property type (residential, commercial, size of space)
  • Any specific challenges or goals for your garden
  • Your preferred timeline for the project
  • Photos of your current space (if possible)

The more details you provide, the better we can tailor our response to your needs.

Do you charge for initial consultations?

We offer complimentary 30-minute phone or video consultations for new clients. For in-person consultations at your property, we charge a $75 fee that is fully credited toward any services you book with us. This fee helps ensure our designers and consultants can dedicate proper time and attention to each potential client.

What's the best way to reach Ecolore for urgent gardening issues?

For urgent matters such as storm damage, hazardous tree situations, or irrigation emergencies, please call our 24/7 emergency line at (555) 123-4568. For non-urgent but time-sensitive issues during business hours, calling our main line at (555) 123-4567 will typically get you the fastest response. Email and contact form submissions are best for general inquiries and non-urgent matters.

Can I visit your office to discuss my gardening project?

While we primarily conduct consultations at client properties where we can assess the actual space, you're welcome to visit our Brooklyn office by appointment. Please call ahead to schedule an office visit as our design team is often out in the field. Our office address is Metrotech Center, Brooklyn, NY 11201, and we're available Monday-Friday 9am-6pm by appointment.

Do you provide gardening services for commercial properties?

Yes, we offer comprehensive gardening and landscaping services for commercial properties including office buildings, retail centers, restaurants, and multi-family residential complexes. Our commercial services include regular maintenance contracts, seasonal displays, rooftop gardens, and complete landscape installations. We have specialized teams and equipment to handle larger-scale projects with minimal disruption to your business operations.

For commercial inquiries, please contact our business development manager directly at commercial@ecolore.com or call (555) 123-4569.

How do I get a quote for gardening services?

We provide free, no-obligation quotes for all our services. The most accurate way to get a quote is to schedule an on-site consultation where we can assess your space and discuss your specific needs. For general estimates:

  • Basic garden maintenance starts at $75 per visit
  • Custom garden design begins at $500 for small spaces
  • Landscape installations vary widely based on materials and scope
  • Irrigation system installation typically ranges from $1,500-$5,000

Complete our contact form with details about your project, and we'll provide a more accurate estimate based on your specific requirements.
